Privileges of Accounting and Developer 
1. Change password
2. Change username
3. Change display name

Privileges of HRD
1. All privileges of Accounting and Developer
2. Can add new user and set their role but cannot modify existing one
3. Freeze an account

Privileges of Admin
1. All privliges of HRD
2. Modify any user's information (password, user name, display name)
3. Freely modify the role of any user (including themselves)
4. Unfreeze and delete an account
